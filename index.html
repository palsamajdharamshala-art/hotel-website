About Pal Samaj Dharmarth Trust
Serving Pilgrims with Comfort, Dignity & Devotion

Founded: 2015

Dharamshala Inaugurated: 13 October 2019

Our Purpose

Pal Samaj Dharmarth Trust was established with a simple yet divine intention — to serve pilgrims, travelers, and families visiting Haridwar with comfort, dignity, and devotion. What began as a community initiative has now become a heartfelt mission — offering affordable, peaceful, and clean accommodation to every visitor, irrespective of background or faith. The trust continues to uphold the values of Seva (सेवा), Simplicity and Sanctity.

Our Story

Born from the collective spirit of Pal Samaj members, this Dharamshala stands as a symbol of unity and compassion. It welcomes not only Pal Samaj families but all devotees who come seeking solace by the holy Ganga. Every brick of this place echoes with prayers, voluntary service, and blessings from countless well-wishers who contributed selflessly to make it a reality.
