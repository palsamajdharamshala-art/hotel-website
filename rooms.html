At Pal Samaj Dharamshala, hospitality is not a business — it’s a tradition of Seva (selfless service). We take pride in providing every guest a peaceful home away from home, nestled in the divine surroundings of Haridwar. Our Dharamshala is known for its unmatched cleanliness, simplicity, and devotion — creating an atmosphere where faith meets comfort. Whether you are a pilgrim, a family traveler, or a first-time visitor to the holy city, we ensure your experience is filled with warmth, purity, and tranquility.

Room Category: Deluxe Double Bed Room
Occupancy: 2 Adults + 1 Child

Every room is thoughtfully designed to provide a serene, relaxing atmosphere. Additional guests can be accommodated upon request without extra cost, as long as disclosed before booking. We strive to make every stay personalized — ensuring your comfort, privacy, and satisfaction at every step.

Comfortable Double Bed

Our rooms are spacious, airy, and equipped with comfortable double beds and clean linens. The peaceful ambience ensures you can unwind completely after exploring temples or attending Ganga Aarti. Experience the calmness of home within a devotional setting.

Attached Bathroom with Geyser

Each room features a private, hygienic bathroom equipped with a modern geyser system. We ensure that you have access to hot water at any hour, so you can begin or end your day with a comfortable and refreshing bath, maintaining complete privacy.

24-Hour Hot & Cold Water

Our Dharamshala offers uninterrupted hot and cold water throughout the day and night. We maintain reliable utilities to guarantee that guests face no inconvenience — a promise that makes us a trusted choice among pilgrims in Haridwar.

Secure Parking Facility

Guests arriving by car can enjoy ample on-premise parking within the Dharamshala premises. The area is monitored and easily accessible, ensuring your vehicle remains safe and close at hand. Travel with peace of mind, knowing your transport is secure.

Housekeeping & Cleanliness

Cleanliness is not just a service — it’s a value we live by. Our dedicated staff provides regular housekeeping to keep rooms fresh, sanitized, and organized. From spotless floors to neatly arranged bedding, every detail reflects our commitment to purity.

24×7 CCTV Security

Your safety is our priority. The entire Dharamshala is under round-the-clock CCTV surveillance and supervised by trusted staff. Families and senior citizens can rest assured knowing they are in a protected and welcoming environment.

What truly sets Pal Samaj Dharamshala apart is our heart-led approach to hospitality. We are guided by tradition, not profit — ensuring every guest is treated with warmth, respect, and sincerity. Our management by Pal Samaj Dharmarth Trust guarantees that all operations remain transparent, with no hidden costs and no commercial distractions. Here, devotion blends seamlessly with comfort, making every stay peaceful, affordable, and spiritually uplifting. Come experience the essence of Haridwar — where every sunrise begins with faith, and every night ends in comfort and gratitude.
